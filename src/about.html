Our Vision

Right from the outset, our vision was inspired by enchanting fairytales and timeless arts. Each creation serves as a canvas, adorned with care and affection, narrating a story that encourages us to live our own version of fairytales.

About Flower Knows

Bringing the fairytale to life worldwide, Flower Knows is an enchanting China-based beauty brand dazzling the globe. Flower Knows offers a fresh take on femininity with unique and gorgeously crafted products that go against the grain. Consisting of transcendent narratives and quality beauty supplies, Flower Knows is on the rise and gaining immense international recognition.

At the brand’s foundation, Flower Knows is all about fairy tales and art pieces rooted in a mission to romanticize the world. Each product is delicately designed and made with love and attention to detail. Flower Knows’ signature aesthetic is evident within each of the brand’s fanciful and fantastical series.
